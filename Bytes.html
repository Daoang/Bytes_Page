About Us

Welcome to the BRILLIANT YOUTH AND TECHNOLOGY ELITE STUDENTS (BYTES) Organization STI Fairview Chapter! We are a passionate community of tech enthusiasts, programmers, and innovators dedicated to creating impactful projects and organizing events that inspire students to grow and excel in the digital era. Together, we cultivate skills, foster collaboration, and empower the next generation of tech leaders.

Our Events

Here are some of the memorable moments from our activities and events at STI Fairview:
ITCS Exhibit – showcasing teamwork, school spirit, and friendly competition.
General Assembly 2025 – uniting students and members to share ideas, plans, and inspiration.
Esports Tournaments – bringing together gamers and tech enthusiasts for exciting battles and camaraderie.
